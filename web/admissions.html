Admissions

Welcome to Insight's School & College! Our admissions process is designed to be simple and transparent.

Admission Process

  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (previous report cards, birth certificate, etc.).
  3. Attend an entrance assessment (if applicable).
  4. Interview with faculty (for higher grades).
  5. Receive admission confirmation and complete the fee payment.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Passport-size Photos
  • Parent/Guardian ID Proof
